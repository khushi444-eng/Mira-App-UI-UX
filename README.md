# Mira-App-UI-UX
DESCRIPTION :
Mira is a simple and calming period tracking app designed to help users understand their bodies without stress or confusion.

It allows users to log their cycles, track symptoms and mood, and receive gentle insights instead of overwhelming medical information.

The app also includes an AI chatbot for everyday questions, a safe anonymous community space, and easy-to-read articles to answer common “why is this happening?” doubts.

Mira focuses on emotional comfort, cultural sensitivity, and making users feel safe while learning about their health.
